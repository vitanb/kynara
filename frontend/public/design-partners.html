Who it's for

The program is built for teams running agents that take consequential actions and have someone who owns that risk. Two profiles fit best: DevOps / SecOps teams whose agents execute remediation (agentic SOC, AI SRE, incident response), and regulated teams (fintech, healthcare, insurance) running internal agents under audit pressure.

✓ Strong fit

  • Agents take real actions with side effects
  • 1–3 agents in or near production
  • Built on LangChain / LangGraph, CrewAI, AutoGen, or MCP
  • A named owner for agent risk (platform / security lead)
  • Can integrate within ~30 days

Not yet a fit

  • Read-only chat / RAG Q&A only
  • Demo or hackathon stage
  • No real tool-calling or side effects
  • No one owns the risk internally
  • 6-month procurement to run a pilot

Questions

Is it really free?

Yes — free through the program and for 12 months after. We're optimizing for feedback and reference outcomes, not revenue, at this stage.

Do I have to be a public reference?

No. A logo, quote, or case study is opt-in and only if Kynara delivered real value. You can participate fully and stay anonymous.

How much work is it on our side?

A 45-minute scoping call, one workflow integrated (~30 days, we pair with you), and a 30-minute biweekly call. That's it.

How is this different from an identity product?

Identity platforms authenticate the agent — who it is. Kynara is the layer above: it decides allow / deny / require-approval on every action, guarantees an agent can't exceed the human who dispatched it, and keeps a tamper-evident log. See how Kynara compares.
